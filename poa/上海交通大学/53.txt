　　大学本科(简称“大学”)及以上学历,学士及以上学位  
　　 Applicants should have at least a Bachelor degree, interpersonal and
communication skills, and good work ethics. SCCP has a number of non-Chinese
faculty members, fulfilling their official duties using English as the working
language. In addition, SCCP hosts international visitors and exchange students
frequently. Proficiency in English language including listening comprehension,
speaking, and writing, is therefore required.  
  
　　The administrative and academic assistant to be hired will assist with
research activities including the preparation of research proposals and
budget, purchase orders, reports, reimbursement, and related items. The
assistant will also help organize scientific meetings and assist faculty
members in managing their travel and other activities, and perform secretarial
duties.
