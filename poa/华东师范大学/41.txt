题 目：A Robust Multivariate EWMA Control Chart for Detecting Sparse Mean Shifts

报告人：华东师范大学 濮晓龙 教授

时 间：2016年7月2日 14:00—15:30

地 点：数学楼629

Abstract: In multivariate statistical process control (MSPC) applications,
process mean shifts sometimes occur in only a few components. Most ofexisting
charts assumed that the multivariate quality characteristics are normally
distributed. Among them, the multivariate LASSO control chartproposed by Zou
and Qiu (2009) has the best overall performance. In this paper, we extend the
classical multivariate LASSO control chart to a robust version thatis
distribution-free under the family of elliptical direction distributions,
indicating that the in-control run length distribution is the same for any
continuous distribution in this family and the control limit can be acquired
from the multivariate standard normal distribution. Simulation results show
that the proposed method is very efficient in detecting various sparse shifts
underheavy-tailed and skewed multivariate distributions.

Key Words: Affine Invariant, Distribution Free, Least Absolute Shrinkage and
Selection Operator, Multivariate Statistical Process Control, Spatial Sign
Test.
